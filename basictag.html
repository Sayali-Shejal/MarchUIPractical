Mahabaleshwar Tourism

Paradise Of Maharashtra

Mahabaleshwar the most popular hill station in Maharashtra is also called as Paradise Of Maharashtra. Mahabaleshwar is located in the Western Ghats also known as Sahyadri, in Satara district of Maharashtra. Mahabaleshwar is one of few evergreen forests of india, which remains green throughout the whole year. The word Mahableshwar has been derived from Sanskrit word meaning 'God of Great Power' (Lord Shiva). Origination is also said to be from the word Mamleshwar which means 'God of Mavalas' who is Lord Shiva. Mahabaleshwar comprises three villages: Malcolm Peth, Old "Kshetra" Mahabaleshwar and part of the Shindola village.

Land Of Strawberries

Mahabaleshwar strawberry contributes to about 85 percent of the total strawberry production in the country. One can visit strawberry farms during the period they are grown between December to April. A trip to Mahabaleshwar would be incomplete without a visit to Strawberry Farm in the land of Strawberries.
